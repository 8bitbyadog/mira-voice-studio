================================================================================
                         MIRA VOICE STUDIO - TRAINING INBOX
================================================================================

Drop your audio files here to train a custom voice model!

SUPPORTED FORMATS:
  • WAV (recommended)
  • MP3
  • M4A
  • FLAC
  • OGG

RECOMMENDATIONS:
  • 10-30 minutes of clear speech works best
  • Use a consistent microphone and recording environment
  • Minimize background noise
  • Speak naturally, as if talking to a friend
  • Include varied sentences (questions, statements, exclamations)

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT:
  1. Open Mira Voice Studio
  2. Go to Train → Import tab
  3. Your files will be automatically detected
  4. The app will process and prepare them for training
  5. You can review clips and start training

TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS:
  • Record in a quiet room with minimal echo
  • Keep a consistent distance from your microphone (~6 inches)
  • Avoid long pauses or excessive "umms" and "uhhs"
  • Speak at your natural pace — don't rush or drag

Need help? Check the app's Help menu or press H in the app.
